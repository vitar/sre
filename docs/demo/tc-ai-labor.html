Events
2012ImageNet breakthrough — deep learning inflection begins. AI capability curve separates from prior trajectory. Labor market appears unaffected. E-debt begins accumulating below detection threshold.
2017Automation reports peak (McKinsey, Oxford). 47% of US jobs at high risk. Distortion visible in research — not yet in allocation. Human capital buffer absorbs gap. Borrowed Life phase begins.
2020COVID accelerates digital displacement — 5 years of adoption in 12 months. Remote-compatible roles insulated; routine physical and cognitive roles exposed. Adaptation gap widens.
2022ChatGPT launches. Signal crosses Gate — balancing feedback reaches allocation logic for the first time at scale. White-collar displacement becomes publicly legible. Stress phase begins.
2024Broad displacement visible: legal, financial analysis, content, coding. Overhead rises — incumbent protection mechanisms activate. Adaptation gap compounding. Carrier population approaching critical zone.
2027Fork point — Branch A: retraining infrastructure reaches scale. E-allocation rises. Carrier population stabilizes above irreversibility threshold. Overhead begins to recede. Recovery trajectory confirmed.
2027Fork point — Branch B: Evolution investment remains blocked. Carrier population crosses irreversibility threshold. Reinforcing loop locks in. Protection overhead ratchets permanently. Structural intervention required.
2030Flow. New labor market equilibrium established. Adaptation costs were high and unevenly distributed — but the system retains Evolution capacity. E-debt closing. Human capital replenishing.
2030Starvation. Reinforcing loop active — rising protection overhead crowds out E-allocation, deepening the adaptation gap, which drives further protection demand. The system cannot self-correct without Code-level intervention.
